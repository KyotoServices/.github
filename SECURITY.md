# Security Policy

We take the security of the KyotoPVP network and our players seriously. Thank you for helping keep
the community safe.

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues, Discord channels, or in-game.**

Instead, report them privately so we can investigate and ship a fix before details become public:

- **Email:** security@kyotopvp.com
- **Discord:** open a ticket at [discord.kyotopvp.com](https://discord.kyotopvp.com) and ask for a staff member from the security team

Please include, where possible:

- A description of the issue and its potential impact
- Steps to reproduce (proof-of-concept, affected version/client, server, or endpoint)
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or recordings

## What to expect

- We aim to **acknowledge** your report within **72 hours**.
- We'll keep you updated on our progress as we validate and remediate.
- With your permission, we're happy to **credit** you once a fix is released.

## Scope

In scope: our public game servers, websites (`*.kyotopvp.com`), the public API, and our Discord tooling.

Out of scope: social-engineering of staff or players, physical attacks, denial-of-service / spam,
and reports from automated scanners without a demonstrated, reproducible impact.

## Safe harbor

We will not pursue or support legal action against researchers who act in good faith, avoid privacy
violations and service disruption, and give us a reasonable chance to remediate before public disclosure.
